Our Product

Introducing The SquawkAv Web Application…A Major Enabler For The SWIM Concept
Squawkings Aviation Software Limited has developed a harmonized process and protocols for exchanging data between multiple States/Territories/International Organizations within and across regions to satisfy this requirement in accordance with the ICAO standards.

With increasing pressure to continuously improve operations at many airports, sharing accurate and timely information is becoming ever more important. With our air traffic control solutions, we are providing a system that support in maintaining a safe and efficient airspace through effective data sharing from the initial reception of flight plan data from the aircraft operator to the simultaneous distribution of this data to their respective ATS units. This requirement stems from the increasing traffic levels crossing FIR boundaries and the need to improve efficiency and accuracy for the ATC providers.

FEATURES
  • Supports the use of the RPL, FPL and AFIL flight plan system.
  • An Automated AIS Flight plan log book.
  • An Automated ADR-16 (Flight movement messages)
  • A complete stop to the use of AFTN/AMHS
  • ATS messages in ICAO, ADEXP, OLDI formats.
  • Support for reception and transmission of other free text message carried by the AFTN
  • The automation of operations count at each airport (Landings, Take-offs, Overfly, Returned flights, Diverted flights, Rejected Takeoffs).
  • The automation of operations count for each sectors (Landings, Take-offs, Overfly, Returned flights, Diverted flights, Rejected Takeoffs).
  • A specially designed User Interface for Enhanced Strategic Planning (ESPL).
  • An automated Radio watch log for the Airspace Users
  • Complete data loss recovery system (storing recorded data on a removable media)
  • Common time synchronization (UTC).
  • Path Integration with other 3rd party software.
  • Meeting future capacity and safety needs in a cost-effective and sustainable way
  • Durable storage, archiving of all received and transmitted messages to meet legal retention obligations;
  • Easy-to-use, friendly multi-window graphic interface.
  • Sophisticated features (Alert Notification)
  • Airline’s fleets database
  • Designed to successfully perform for both large scale ACC centers and small/medium airports
  • SquawkAv Flight plan processing system is designed to swiftly rationalize the reception, initial processing and distribution of flight plans.
